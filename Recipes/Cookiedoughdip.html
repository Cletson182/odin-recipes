Cookie Dough Dip

Cookie dough in dip form! Made with cream cheese and chocolate chips, this dream, creamy Cookie Dough dip is whipped up in a flash. Servie with pretzels, graham crackers, anything really!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup semisweet mini chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, beat the butter, cream cheese, sugars, vanilla, and salt on low until smooth, about 2-4 minutes
  2. Fold in the mini chocolate chips until evenly distributed in the dip
  3. Serve with dippers like pretzels, green apples or chocolate graham crackers, waffle cones, or get really wild and try a sweet and salty combo with corn chip dippers!
